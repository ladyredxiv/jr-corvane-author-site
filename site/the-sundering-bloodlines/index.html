The series

A world still living in the shadow of a failed magical war.

The Sundering Bloodlines is an adult epic fantasy trilogy about inherited power, buried history, and the people forced to pay for the lies that built their kingdoms.

Ancient bindings are weakening. Forgotten bloodlines are awakening. Each revelation threatens the institutions founded to conceal the truth—and each victory carries consequences far beyond those who won it.

These are stories of old magic, political consequence, broken loyalties, and wonder surviving in the darkness.
