The story

The legend of the Sundering has held the kingdom together for generations.

In the exile settlement of Kelmoor, Saphielle Caina has spent her life hiding the mark on her skin. When that mark answers an ancient power, she is driven from the only home she has known and into a conflict older than the kingdom itself.

A retired hunter searches for the daughter stolen by a rising cult. A loyal servant of the Concord uncovers the foundations of its most sacred deception. And beyond the storm-scarred Greywound, something bound for generations has begun to reach back.

To stop it, they must uncover what truly happened at the Sundering—and decide what they are willing to sacrifice when the truth demands another price.
