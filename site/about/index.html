The author

Stories shaped by ancient histories, costly magic, and the weight of consequence.

J.R. Corvane writes adult epic fantasy about kingdoms built on old decisions and the people forced to live with their cost.

Corvane’s work brings together mythic scale, political pressure, moral ambiguity, and the stubborn survival of hope in dark places. These stories are for readers who want every relic to carry a history, every crown to conceal a debt, and every victory to ask for something in return.

Blood of the Bound, the first novel in The Sundering Bloodlines, is currently in edits.

“Epic fantasy often promises battles. J.R. Corvane promises consequence.”
